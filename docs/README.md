# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

Welcome! This README introduces and links to OctoAcme's standardized project management processes for cross-functional teams.

## Project Management Process Summary

OctoAcme relies on customer-centric, iterative delivery, clear roles, and regular communication to drive project success. The approach is structured around six core phases:

- **Initiation:** Define business need, align stakeholders, and sketch a lightweight plan with success metrics and resource requirements.
- **Planning:** Break down work into shippable increments, map milestones and dependencies, identify risks, and create a prioritized backlog with clear acceptance criteria.
- **Execution & Tracking:** Use project boards and regular review cycles to maintain quality, visibility, and progress toward milestones. Daily standups and weekly syncs keep the team aligned.
- **Risk & Communication:** Track risks proactively, communicate status to stakeholders at regular intervals, and escalate blockers through defined escalation paths.
- **Release & Deployment:** Deliver with safety checks, automated testing, rollback strategies, and comprehensive status visibility to production systems.
- **Retrospective & Improvement:** Capture lessons learned, act on team feedback, and continuously evolve processes to drive better outcomes.

## Key Principles

- **Customer-first:** Prioritize customer value and usability in all decisions.
- **Iterative delivery:** Deliver small, testable increments to enable rapid feedback and learning.
- **Clear ownership:** Each project has a named Project Manager and Product Lead with defined responsibilities.
- **Data-informed:** Measure impact and iterate based on evidence and metrics.
- **Psychological safety:** Encourage feedback, learning, and blameless retrospectives.

## Core Roles

- **Project Manager (PM):** Coordinates delivery, manages schedules, risks, and communications.
- **Product Manager (PdM):** Defines outcomes, prioritizes the backlog, and measures success.
- **Developers:** Implement features, collaborate on design, and ensure testability and quality.
- **QA/Testing:** Validates quality and ensures acceptance criteria are met.
- **Stakeholders:** Provide inputs, approvals, and business context.

## Communication Cadence

- **Daily:** Team standups (15 min) — progress, blockers, dependencies.
- **Weekly:** PM + PdM alignment sync and project status review.
- **Bi-weekly or per sprint:** Delivery team planning and review sessions.
- **Monthly or milestone-based:** Stakeholder updates and executive briefings.
- **Ad-hoc:** Escalations and incident communication as needed.
